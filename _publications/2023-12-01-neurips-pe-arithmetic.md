This work investigates the possibility of composing parameter-efficient modules (PEMs) through arithmetic operations such as addition, subtraction, and interpolation. We demonstrate that these modules can be flexibly combined to achieve versatile model adaptations, opening new possibilities for modular and compositional fine-tuning approaches.
