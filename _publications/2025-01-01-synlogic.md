SynLogic is a framework that synthesizes verifiable reasoning data at scale, enabling effective learning of logical reasoning capabilities in large language models. The approach creates high-quality training data that can be automatically verified, addressing the challenge of acquiring large-scale reasoning data for training LLMs.
