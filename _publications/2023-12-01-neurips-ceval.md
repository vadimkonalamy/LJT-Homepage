C-Eval is a comprehensive benchmark designed to evaluate the knowledge and reasoning capabilities of foundation models across multiple disciplines and difficulty levels in Chinese. The suite covers a wide range of subjects from humanities to science and engineering, providing a thorough assessment of model performance in Chinese language understanding.
