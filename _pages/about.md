## About Me

I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.hkust.edu.hk/), supervised by Professor [Junxian He](https://junxianh.github.io/). My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning, with particular interests in:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**

I graduated from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024 with a B.Eng. degree.
